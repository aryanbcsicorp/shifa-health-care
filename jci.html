    JCI

    we are proud to announce that we have achieved accreditation from the Joint Commission International (JCI), a globally recognized leader in healthcare quality and patient safety standards. This prestigious accreditation reaffirms our commitment to providing world-class healthcare services and upholding the highest standards of excellence in patient care, safety, and quality improvement.

    What is JCI Accreditation?

    JCI accreditation is awarded to healthcare organizations that demonstrate compliance with rigorous standards for patient care, clinical outcomes, facility management, and continuous quality improvement. As a JCI-accredited institution has undergone thorough evaluation and met stringent criteria across various domains, including:

    • Patient Rights and Responsibilities
    • Access to Care and Continuity of Care
    • Patient and Family Education
    • Infection Prevention and Control
    • Governance and Leadership
    • Facility Management and Safety
    • Clinical Care
    1.Benefits of JCI Accreditation:
    • Enhanced Patient Safety: JCI accreditation demonstrates our adherence to international standards for patient safety, infection control, and risk management practices, ensuring a safe and secure environment for our patients.
    • Quality Improvement: JCI accreditation fosters a culture of continuous quality improvement by providing frameworks, benchmarks, and best practices to enhance clinical outcomes, patient satisfaction, and organizational efficiency.
    • International Recognition: JCI accreditation is recognized worldwide as a symbol of healthcare quality and excellence, enhancing our reputation and credibility as a trusted healthcare provider.
    • Professional Development: JCI accreditation promotes professional development and accountability among our staff, encouraging ongoing education, training, and collaboration to maintain high standards of care.
    • Patient-Centered Care: JCI accreditation emphasizes patient-centered care principles, including respect for patient rights, communication, and involvement in decision-making, ensuring that patients receive compassionate, individualized care.
    • Community Confidence: JCI accreditation instills confidence and trust in our patients, their families, and the community we serve, affirming our commitment to providing safe, high-quality healthcare services.
    2.Our Commitment to JCI Standards:
    • We adhere to evidence-based clinical practices, protocols, and guidelines endorsed by JCI to ensure the delivery of safe, effective, and patient-centered care.
    • We continuously monitor and evaluate our performance against JCI standards, conducting regular audits, assessments, and quality improvement initiatives to identify areas for enhancement and drive organizational excellence.
    • We engage our staff, patients, and stakeholders in the accreditation process, fostering a culture of transparency, accountability, and collaboration to achieve and sustain JCI accreditation.
