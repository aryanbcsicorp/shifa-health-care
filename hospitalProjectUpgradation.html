    Hospital Project - Greenfield & Upgradation Projects

    Our Hospital Project Division Will help you upgrade your existing Hospital or set up a new green field one in full compliance with NABH &JCI.Our team of Hospital Administrators,Project Manager,Architects,Planners and Equipment,Spacialist the largest in the country today.

    I Project provides an end solution right form establishing the Feasibility of the hospital up to commmising it,in full compalianve with all prestiigious National & international quality accreditations like the NABH,the national Accreditation Board For Hospitals and HealthCare Providers and the JCI,joint commission International.

    Our Service would include:

    1.Market Feasibility Studies to help you geta better understanding of:
    • Existing medical facilities available in the primary and secondary catachment areas of yor Hospital.
    • Need gaps if any in the existing medical facilities which the new hospital can help fulfil.
    • Opinion of Healthcare Practitioners in the area on the need and the set of sepacialities that would work given the needs.
    • Modalities the new hospital should adopt which will potentially attract patients from nerby areas in the state
    • For More Details on market Feasibility Studies and Project
    2.Detailed Project Report(DPR),to provide you:
    • cost of the project-Land,Building,Equipment & Services.
    • Cost of running the hospital - Cost of Operations.
    • Breakeven Levels of Operation for acheiving Profitability
    • Profit & loss and cash Flow Projections over a 10-year period.
    3.Equipment Planning,Procurement,Installation and Commissioning:
    • Developing full list of eqipment,along with the Budget
    • Selecting right equipment based on price,end user requirment,upgradation,availability of technical support,accessories and spare parts.
    • Procurement support,including tender documents.
    • Installing and commission support.
