    National Accreditation Board for Hospitals & Healthcare Providers (NABH) Accreditation

    we are proud to be accredited by the National Accreditation Board for Hospitals & Healthcare Providers (NABH), a prestigious accreditation body that sets rigorous standards for healthcare quality and patient safety. NABH accreditation is a testament to our commitment to delivering excellence in healthcare services and adhering to the highest standards of patient care

    Benefits of NABH Accreditation


    • Enhanced Patient Safety: NABH accreditation signifies our adherence to stringent standards for patient safety, infection control, and risk management practices, ensuring a safe and secure environment for our patients.
    • Quality Improvement: NABH accreditation encourages continuous quality improvement through the implementation of evidence-based practices, clinical protocols, and performance monitoring mechanisms, leading to better patient outcomes and satisfaction.
    • Patient-Centered Care: Our accreditation reflects our dedication to patient-centered care, emphasizing compassionate communication, respect for patient rights, and involvement of patients and their families in decision-making processes.
    • Professional Development: NABH accreditation promotes professional development and accountability among our healthcare staff, fostering a culture of learning, innovation, and excellence in healthcare delivery.
    • Recognition of Excellence: Achieving NABH accreditation demonstrates our commitment to excellence and distinguishes us as a healthcare provider of choice, instilling confidence and trust in our patients, their families, and the community we serve.

    Our Commitment to NABH Standards


    • Clinical Governance: We have robust clinical governance structures in place to ensure the highest standards of clinical care, patient safety, and ethical practices.
    • Infrastructure & Facilities: Our hospital infrastructure and facilities meet NABH requirements for safety, cleanliness, accessibility, and functionality, providing a conducive environment for healing and recovery.
    • Clinical Processes: We adhere to evidence-based clinical practices, protocols, and guidelines endorsed by NABH, promoting standardized and efficient delivery of healthcare services.
    • Patient Rights & Responsibilities: We respect and uphold the rights and dignity of our patients, empowering them to participate in their care, make informed decisions, and voice any concerns or grievances they may have.
    • Continuous Quality Improvement: We are committed to ongoing quality improvement initiatives, including regular audits, performance evaluations, and feedback mechanisms, to identify areas for enhancement and ensure the delivery of high-quality care.
